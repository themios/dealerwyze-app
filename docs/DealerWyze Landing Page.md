
# DealerWyze Landing Page + Demo Brief

## 1. Strategic Diagnosis

DealerWyze already does several things right:

- The hero clearly targets independent dealers.
- The site identifies real pains: fragmented leads, overnight misses, weak follow-up.
- The product breadth is strong: Gmail/IMAP import, Today list, AI features, pricing intelligence, BHPH, reviews, appointments, SMS, analytics.

The problem is not lack of functionality. The problem is conversion clarity.

The homepage currently leads with a broad promise and then expands into a large feature inventory. That creates three risks:

- The visitor has to work too hard to understand the economic outcome.
- The site tries to sell too many products at once: CRM, AI assistant, pricing tool, BHPH, bookkeeping, reviews, fax, etc.
- The first conversion action is "Start Free" before the site has fully earned trust with quantified proof.

---

## 2. Positioning

**Primary promise:** Stop losing high-intent dealership leads after hours, between follow-ups, and across disconnected inboxes.

**Secondary promise:** DealerWyze gives independent dealers one place to capture, respond to, and work every lead until it becomes an appointment or sale.

**Economic framing:** If one missed lead can cost hundreds or thousands in gross profit, then slow response and broken follow-up are not admin problems. They are revenue leaks.

### Section I — FAQ

**Does this work with Gmail or IMAP?**
Yes. DealerWyze connects directly to your Gmail or any IMAP inbox and automatically imports leads into customer records. No manual entry required.

**Can I connect AutoTrader and CarGurus lead emails?**
Yes. Lead notification emails from AutoTrader, CarGurus, and most major listing sites are automatically parsed and matched to the right vehicle and customer.

**Can I use DealerWyze by myself without a team?**
Absolutely. The Today list and single-screen workflow are specifically designed for one-person and small-team lots. No admin overhead or complex setup.

**Does it support BHPH workflows?**
Yes. DealerWyze includes a Buy Here Pay Here module with payment tracking, customer payment history, and automated payment reminders.

**What happens after beta?**
Beta users receive advance notice before any paid rollout. There will be no surprise charges. You will have time to decide whether to continue with a paid plan.

## 4. What to Remove or De-emphasize

Do not lead with the full feature catalog. Do not make pricing intelligence, fax, bookkeeping, reviews, AI voice, and BHPH all compete on the first visit. Do not use "Start Free" as the dominant CTA before the visitor understands the payoff.

**The homepage should sell one thing: DealerWyze stops lead leakage for independent dealers.**

Everything else is supporting evidence.

**Demo goal:** Show one complete, believable workflow. Do not tour the entire app.

**Demo title:** How DealerWyze Stops a Lead From Falling Through the Cracks

*Voiceover: "Now you can see where leads came from, who followed up, which vehicle they wanted, and what is stuck. That means faster recovery, better accountability, and fewer silent losses."*

### Scene 7 — Close (2:40-3:00)
*Voiceover: "If you run an independent lot and you are tired of losing leads between calls, texts, emails, and memory, DealerWyze was built for that exact problem. Request beta access and see it on your own workflow."*

---

## 7. Demo Production Rules

- Use one real lead example, not a fictional feature montage.
- Keep the screen zoomed in; mobile-first feel.
- Put captions on every scene.
- Show speed: import, find, call, log, next step.
- Do not narrate every feature.
- Do not use vague claims like "streamline your business."
