Who is DealerWyze for?

Independent and used-car dealers who are running daily operations through spreadsheets, notepads, random texts, inboxes, and disconnected tools.

Is DealerWyze just a CRM?

No. CRM is one part of the system. DealerWyze also connects inventory, vehicle-linked conversations, BHPH, receipt OCR, bookkeeping, pricing, advertising, analytics, and automated importing.

Do I need to replace everything on day one?

No. Start by replacing lead tracking and follow-ups. From there, inventory, BHPH, receipts, and marketing connect naturally around the same customer and vehicle records.

Can I use it if my leads come through Gmail or marketplace emails?

Yes. DealerWyze is designed to import leads from Gmail and IMAP inboxes and parse common dealership lead emails into organized customer and vehicle records.

What should I do first: start free or book a workflow review?

Start free if you want to test the system directly. Book a workflow review if you want help mapping your current process into DealerWyze before switching.
