## Quick Tips

- **Respond fast** — The faster you reply to leads, the higher your chance of closing the deal
- **Use automation** — Let sequences handle follow-ups while you focus on hot leads
- **Check Today daily** — New leads and tasks arrive throughout the day
- **Keep inventory current** — Fresh photos and status updates increase lead interest
- **Review lead intent** — Prioritize hot (high-intent) leads over warm or cold
