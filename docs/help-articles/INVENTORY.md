# Managing Your Inventory

## What is Inventory?

Your **Inventory** (or **Listings** in RealtyWyze) is the collection of vehicles or properties you're selling. The system tracks:
- Vehicle/property details (make, model, price, location, etc.)
- Photos and videos
- Condition (available, pending, sold, staging)
- Lead interest (who's looking at it)
- Pricing and market intelligence

---

## Accessing Inventory

Click **Inventory** (dealers) or **Listings** (RealtyWyze) in the left sidebar.

---

## Page Layout

### Filters & Sorting

**Status filter chips** (left side):
- **All** — Show all vehicles
- **Available** — Ready to sell
- **Pending** — Awaiting sale completion
- **Sold** — Already sold
- **Staging** — Being prepared for listing

**Sort dropdown** (right side):
- Newest first
- Price ascending
- Price descending
- Year descending
- Oldest first

### Top Right Buttons

**"Add Inventory"** (dealers) or **"Add Listing"** (RealtyWyze)
- Opens form to add a new vehicle/property
- Or opens **Vehicle Intake Sheet** for quick data entry

**"Sync Inventory"** — Sync vehicles from external sources (dealer feeds, MLS, etc.)

**"Run Market Intelligence"** (admin dealers only) — Generate market analysis for your lot

---

## How to Add a New Vehicle

1. Click **"Add Inventory"** in the top right
2. Choose entry method:
   - **Manual entry** — Type details into form
   - **Scan** — Take a photo of the vehicle or a document
   - **Paste** — Paste details from text/email
3. Fill in details:
   - Year, make, model
   - Price
   - Stock number
   - Condition
   - Photos/video
   - Features (color, mileage, transmission, etc.)
4. Click **Save**
5. Vehicle appears in your **Inventory** list

---

## Vehicle Status

Each vehicle has a status that tells you its availability:
- **Available** — Ready for sale, leads can inquire
- **Pending** — Deal in progress, likely won't sell to others
- **Sold** — Already sold, no longer available
- **Staging** — Being prepared, photos being uploaded

Update status as deals progress.

---

## Demand Signals & Lead Interest

The system shows you:
- **How many leads** are interested in each vehicle
- **Demand signal** — How hot this vehicle is (high demand = sell faster)
- **Days on market** — How long it's been listed

Use this to price competitively and prioritize sales efforts.

---

## Market Intelligence

When enabled, the system shows:
- **Comparable vehicles** in your market
- **Suggested pricing** based on condition and demand
- **Pricing trends** (is demand going up or down?)
- **Competitor pricing** (what others are asking)

Use this to price your vehicles competitively.

---

## Tips

- **Keep photos current** — Fresh photos increase interest
- **Price competitively** — Use market intelligence to stay in line with demand
- **Update status promptly** — When a vehicle sells, mark it as sold so leads see accurate inventory
- **Add detailed features** — The more details you provide, the easier leads can find what they want
