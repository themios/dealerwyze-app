# Web Leads

## What are web leads?

**Web Leads** are inquiries that come through your public website form. They need to be reviewed and imported into your CRM to start working with them.

---

## Accessing Web Leads

1. Click **Web Leads** in the left sidebar
2. You'll see a badge showing how many new web inquiries arrived in the last 7 days

---

## Web Leads Page Layout

### Tab Filters (4 tabs)
- **All** — Show all inquiries
- **New** — Unreviewed inquiries (red badge if any)
- **Imported** — Already added to your Leads
- **Archived** — Dismissed inquiries

Each tab shows the count of inquiries.

### Each Inquiry Card Shows:

1. **Header row:**
   - Status dot (green=new, blue=imported, gray=archived)
   - Name
   - Phone (clickable tel: link)
   - Email (clickable mailto: link)
   - Timestamp (when inquiry arrived)

2. **Vehicle row** (if they mentioned a vehicle):
   - Car icon
   - Year, Make, Model
   - Stock number (if you carry used inventory)
   - Link to that vehicle in your inventory

3. **Message row:**
   - Their inquiry message (quoted)
   - **"Show more"** button if the message is long
   - Full message displays when expanded

4. **Action buttons** (context-aware by status):
   - **Not imported yet:**
     - **"Import as Lead"** (primary, blue) — Add to your Leads and start working with them
     - **"Archive"** (secondary) — Dismiss without importing
     - **"Delete"** (text link) — Remove permanently
   - **Already imported:**
     - **"Re-import"** (secondary) — Add again (duplicate)
     - **"Archive"** (secondary)
     - **"Delete"** (text link)
   - **Archived:**
     - **"Re-import"** (secondary) — Unarchive and start working
     - **"Restore"** (secondary) — Same as re-import
     - **"Delete"** (text link)

---

## How to Import a Web Lead

1. Open the **New** tab to see unreviewed inquiries
2. Find the inquiry you want to work with
3. Click **"Import as Lead"**
4. You'll be taken to the **Add Lead** form with their info pre-filled:
   - Name
   - Phone
   - Email
5. Add any additional details (vehicle interests, notes, etc.)
6. Click **Save**
7. Lead is now in your **Leads** list and appears on **Today**

---

## How to Archive a Web Lead

If you don't want to work with an inquiry:
1. Click **"Archive"** on the inquiry card
2. It moves to the **Archived** tab
3. You can still **"Restore"** it later if you change your mind

---

## How to Delete a Web Lead

If you want to remove permanently:
1. Click **"Delete"** on the inquiry card
2. Confirm the deletion
3. Inquiry is permanently removed (cannot be recovered)

---

## Tips

- **Review regularly** — Check the **New** tab daily to find fresh opportunities
- **Quick import** — Importing pre-fills the form with their details, saving you time
- **Keep context** — The vehicle row shows what they're interested in, so you know what to offer
- **Archive strategically** — Archive (don't delete) inquiries you might revisit later
