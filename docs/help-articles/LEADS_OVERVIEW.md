# Managing Leads

## What are leads?

Leads are potential customers you're tracking in your CRM. They include contact information, communication history, vehicle interests, and intent scoring to help you prioritize follow-ups.

---

## How to add a new lead

1. Go to **Leads** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **Add Lead** button (Plus icon) in the top right
3. Choose from the menu:
   - **Add manually** — Enter name, phone, email, and vehicle details directly
   - **Scan lead** — Use your phone camera to scan a business card (OCR)
   - **Paste lead** — Paste contact information from text (email, text message, etc.)
   - **Import CSV** — Upload a spreadsheet with multiple leads

Once added, the lead appears in your Leads list and you can start working with them.

---

## How to view your leads

1. Go to **Leads** in the left sidebar
2. The page shows all your leads in a list
3. Use the tabs to organize:
   - **List** — Default view, all leads as a list
   - **Pipeline** — Organize leads by sales stage (pipeline)
   - **Segments** — Group leads by criteria (repeat customers, hot leads, etc.)
   - **Archived** — View archived leads (when viewing archived)

Click any lead to open their profile and see full history, activities, and options to contact them.

---

## How to find a lead

**In the Leads page:**
- Use the search box to find leads by name, phone, or email
- Filter by pipeline stage or segment

**On Today page:**
- New leads appear in the center column, sorted by priority
- Use filter chips (Hot, Warm, Repeat, etc.) to narrow down

---

## Lead Intent Scoring

Your CRM automatically scores leads based on:
- How quickly they replied to you
- Repeat visits to your website
- Specificity of their vehicle interests
- Communication frequency

**Color-coded badges:**
- **HOT** (red/orange) — High intent, respond immediately
- **WARM** (yellow) — Medium intent, follow up today
- **COLD** (gray) — Low intent, less urgent

---

## How to work with a lead on Today

1. Go to **Today** in the left sidebar
2. Look for the lead in the center **Activity Feed** column (sorted by priority)
3. Click the lead card to open their profile, or use quick actions:
   - **Call** — Dial their phone number (tel: link)
   - **Text** — Send an SMS message
   - **Schedule Appointment** — Pick a date/time for a follow-up call
   - **Done** — Mark as handled (removes from Today)
   - **Follow up** — Snooze (Tomorrow, 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks; optionally add to Google Calendar)
   - **Dismiss** — Archive (removes from active view)

---

## Automation & Sequences

If a lead is enrolled in an automation sequence, you'll see:
- **"AI working · [Sequence Name]"** badge with active indicator
- **Next step due** timestamp

**Actions:**
- **Pause** — Temporarily stop automation
- **Resume** — Restart a paused automation
- **Stop** — Cancel automation for this lead

You can also re-enroll a lead once a sequence completes.

---

## Repeat Leads

If a lead has purchased from you before, they'll show a **"Repeat lead"** badge. These are warm leads — treat them as higher priority.

---

## Lead Unsubscribe Status

Check for unsubscribe badges:
- **Email** — Lead has unsubscribed from email
- **SMS** — Lead has unsubscribed from text messages

Respect these preferences and use alternative contact methods or reach out to re-engage.
