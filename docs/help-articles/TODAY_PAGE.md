# Using Today Page

## What is the Today page?

The **Today** page is your activity dashboard. It shows everything you need to work on right now:
- New leads waiting for a response
- Tasks due today
- Customers waiting for follow-up
- Completed voice calls needing action
- Vehicle matches to your want-list

The page is automatically sorted by priority — the most important items appear first.

---

## Accessing Today

Click **Today** in the left sidebar. The page updates in real-time as new leads and activities arrive.

---

## Page Layout

### Top Bar
- **Left:** Gmail sync button (sync recent emails as leads)
- **Right:** 
  - Calendar icon → Go to `/calendar`
  - Receipt icon → Scan expense receipts

### KPI Strip (5 Metrics)
Shows counts of items waiting for you:
- **New Leads** — Inbound emails, not yet addressed
- **Appointments** — Appointments scheduled today
- **Voice Calls** — Completed calls needing follow-up
- **Waiting** — Outbound calls/texts awaiting response
- **Overdue** — Tasks past their due date

### 3-Column Layout (Desktop)

**Left Column — Intelligence:**
- **Dealer Brief** — Your dealer score and today's trends
- **Recent Reviews** — Google/Facebook reviews (last 30 days)
- **Onboarding Checklist** — Progress on setup (add lead, connect email, etc.)

**Center Column — Activity Feed:**
- **Filter Chips** — Filter by lead quality (Hot, Warm, Repeat, Appointments, Phone, No Automation)
- **Activity Queue** — Your prioritized to-do list with quick actions
- **Bulk Actions** — When you select multiple items, options to park, work, or archive

**Right Column — Tasks:**
- **To-Do List** — Open tasks (max 50), sorted by priority
- Click any task to snooze (reschedule) or mark complete

---

## Working with New Leads

Each new lead card shows:
- **Name** (clickable to open full profile)
- **Source** (CarGurus, Zillow, Facebook, etc.)
- **Lead quality score** (quality of their information)
- **Intent tier badge** (HOT/WARM/COLD if set)
- **Why they ranked here** (2-3 reasons from the queue)

### Quick Actions:
- **Call** — Dial their phone number
- **Text** — Send an SMS message
- **Schedule Appointment** — Pick a date/time, optionally add to Google Calendar
- **Done** — Mark addressed (removes from Today)
- **Follow up** — Snooze (Tomorrow/3 days/1 week/2 weeks)
- **Dismiss** — Archive (removes from active view)

### Automation Status:
If the lead is enrolled in an automation sequence:
- **"AI working · [Sequence Name]"** — Sequence is active
- **"Paused · [Sequence Name]"** — Sequence is paused
- **"Sequence done"** — Completed; you can re-enroll
- **Pause/Resume/Stop buttons** — Control the automation

---

## Responding to Waiting Leads

**Waiting** items are outbound calls/texts you sent >24 hours ago that haven't been responded to. Click the card to:
- **Call** — Follow up with a call
- **Text** — Send another message
- **Schedule Appointment** — Propose a time to talk
- **Done** — Mark resolved
- **Follow up** — Snooze for later

---

## Vehicle Matches & Appointments

**Vehicle Matches** appear when a customer's want-list matches a vehicle in your inventory.

**Appointment Requests** appear when a customer requests to schedule a showing or call. Confirm or reschedule.

---

## Focus Mode

If you want to focus on a single lead without distractions:
- Look for the lead card
- Click the lead name to open **Focus Mode**
- Full-screen view of just that lead, with all available actions
- Click X to exit

---

## Motivational Message

A rotating daily message appears to keep you motivated. Examples:
- "Speed wins. Reply first, earn the sale."
- "Follow up today. Win tomorrow."
- "Yes or no — not maybe. Follow up."

---

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Cmd/Ctrl + Enter** — Send message (in message composer)

---

## Filters

Use filter chips to narrow down your queue:
- **Hot** — High-intent leads (respond immediately)
- **Warm** — Medium-intent leads
- **Repeat** — Customers who bought before
- **Appointment** — Appointment-related activities
- **Phone** — Voice/call activities
- **Silent 7d** — No activity in 7+ days
- **No Automation** — Not enrolled in sequences

---

## Tips

- **Respond fast** — The faster you reply, the higher your chance of closing
- **Use sequences** — Let automation handle follow-ups while you focus on hot leads
- **Mark done when handled** — Keeps your Today list clean and focused
- **Check periodically** — New leads arrive throughout the day
