Why Are Your Best Leads Going Quiet? (Hint: It’s Not Your Pitch.)

When good leads go quiet, it’s tempting to blame the pitch or the price. Often the real reason is **speed**. The buyer was ready to talk. They contacted two or three dealers. The one who replied first with the right car and a clear next step got the conversation. The others got silence. Your pitch might be fine. Your response time wasn’t.

Small lots are especially vulnerable. Leads land in different places - Gmail, AutoTrader, CarGurus, texts - and there’s no single view of who just came in.
