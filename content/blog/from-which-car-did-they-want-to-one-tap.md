From “Which Car Did They Want?” to One Tap: How One List Changed Our Lot.

“Which car did they want?” It’s the question that kills momentum. You remember the customer. You don’t remember the vehicle. You scroll through texts and emails trying to piece it together. By the time you find it, the buyer may have already moved on. That was our lot - until we had **one list** where every lead was tied to the car from day one.

DealerWyze made that possible. Lead emails from CarGurus, AutoTrader, and more import into one place automatically. Each lead is a contact with the vehicle already linked from our synced inventory. We reply with one-tap templates that use the real vehicle and real listing link. We log a call in 10 seconds. Response time is tracked so we know when we’re hitting the under-60-second benchmark. For our Buy Here Pay Here side, the same app handles loans, payments, and reminders. One list didn’t just organize us. It made us the dealer who responds first - with the right car.
