Under 60 Seconds: The Lead-Response Rule Small Dealers Can Finally Hit

The lead-response rule is simple: **under 60 seconds**. The buyer is comparison-shopping. The first dealer to answer with the right car and a clear next step gets the conversation. Yet most small dealers break that rule every day - not because they don’t care, but because they don’t have a system that makes it possible. Leads are buried in Gmail, mixed with texts and voicemails; by the time they find the lead and figure out which vehicle it was, the window has closed.

Breaking the rule has a cost. Slow response means lost deals. It also means no visibility: you don’t know your average response time, so you can’t improve it. You’re guessing.
