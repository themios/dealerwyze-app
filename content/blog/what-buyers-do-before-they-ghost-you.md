What’s the One Thing Buyers Do Before They Ghost You?

They **contact someone else**. The buyer who inquired about your car is often texting or emailing two or three dealers at the same time. The first one to answer clearly, with the right vehicle and a real next step, gets the conversation. The others get ghosted. It’s not always about price or inventory. It’s about who responded first.

If your leads are going quiet, the one thing to fix is **response time**. The industry benchmark is under 60 seconds. Getting there isn’t about working harder; it’s about having a system that makes a fast reply possible. DealerWyze is built for that. You pick a first-contact template and send in one tap. The app records when you replied and tracks response time across every channel. You see green (under 60 sec), yellow (1–5 min), red (over 5 min) in your analytics - so you know exactly where you stand.
