From 3 Days to 3 Minutes: How We Reply to Every Lead Before They Shop Elsewhere.

We used to reply in days. A lead would come in Monday. We’d find it in Gmail Tuesday, remember the vehicle Wednesday, and send a reply Thursday. By then the buyer had often already chosen another dealer. The fix wasn’t more hustle. It was **one place** where every lead lands the moment it arrives, with a reply we could send in minutes - not days.

DealerWyze made that possible. Lead emails from CarGurus, AutoTrader, and more import automatically into one list. Each lead is a contact with the car already linked from our inventory. When a new lead appears on the Today list, we open it, pick a first-contact template (vehicle, price, real listing link), and send. First response in three minutes is normal. The app records when we replied and tracks response time across every channel. Analytics show green (under 5 min), yellow (5–30), red (over 30). We’re not guessing anymore. We’re measuring. Optional AI (Lead Scanner, 24/7 voice) captures leads from photos and after-hours calls so we can reply fast no matter how they came in. We went from 3 days to 3 minutes - and we reply before they shop elsewhere. DealerWyze is the platform that gives small used car dealers that speed.
