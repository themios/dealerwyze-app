How to Be the Dealer Who Responded at 10:01 (Not the One Who Waited Until Morning)

The lead came in at 10 p.m. One dealer replied at 10:01 with the right car and a clear next step. By morning, the buyer had already moved forward with them. The dealer who waited until morning was left with a cold thread. The difference wasn’t luck. It was **having a system** that makes a 10:01 reply possible.

That system is one place where the lead appears the moment it arrives, already tied to the vehicle, with a reply you can send in one tap. DealerWyze is built for exactly that. Lead emails from CarGurus, AutoTrader, and other sources import automatically into one list. You see the new lead, the vehicle, and the customer in one view. You pick a first-contact template (e.g. virtual appointment + delivery + financing), customize if you want, and send. The link in your message goes to the actual car listing from your synced inventory. The app records your response and tracks how fast you replied. If you’re not at your desk, the optional AI voice agent can answer after-hours calls and create the lead for you so you can follow up first thing. Either way, you’re the dealer who responded - not the one who left it until morning.

DealerWyze gives small used car dealers the same advantage the big players have: speed and consistency, without enterprise complexity. Be the one who responded at 10:01.
