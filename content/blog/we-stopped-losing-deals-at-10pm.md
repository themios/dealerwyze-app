We Stopped Losing Deals at 10 p.m. Here’s the One Change That Fixed It.

We were losing deals we never knew we had. A lead would come in at 10 p.m. We’d see it in the morning, reply, and get nothing back. They’d already bought from the dealer who responded at 10:01. The one change that fixed it wasn’t working later or hiring more people. It was **one place** where every lead lands the moment it arrives, with a reply we could send in one tap - even at 10 p.m.

DealerWyze is that place. We connected our Gmail once; CarGurus, AutoTrader, and other lead emails started importing automatically. New leads show up on the Today list with the vehicle already linked. We pick a first-contact template (e.g. virtual walkthrough + delivery + financing), hit send. The link in the message goes to the actual car listing. The app records when we replied. We added the optional AI voice agent so after-hours callers get captured too - we follow up first thing with the full transcript. We didn’t add headcount. We added a system.
