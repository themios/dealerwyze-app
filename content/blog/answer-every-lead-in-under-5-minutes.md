How to Answer Every Lead in Under 60 Seconds (Without Living in Your Inbox)

Answering every lead in under 60 seconds sounds impossible if “answering” means living in your inbox - switching between Gmail, texts, and lead portals, copying names and vehicles, and typing each reply from scratch. For a small lot, that’s a recipe for burnout and still-missed leads.

The alternative is **one place** where leads land the moment they come in, already tied to the right vehicle, with reply templates that are personal but fast. DealerWyze does that. Connect your inbox once; CarGurus, AutoTrader, Cars For Sale, and other lead emails import automatically. Each lead becomes a contact with the car linked from your synced inventory. When a new lead appears on your Today list, you open it, pick a first-contact template (e.g. virtual walkthrough + delivery + financing), and send. The template fills in name, vehicle, price, and the actual listing link. You’re not living in Gmail - you’re working from one list, in one app, and replying in under 60 seconds without typing essays. Two-way SMS and email keep the conversation in the same thread. Response time is tracked across every channel (SMS, email, call) so you see green/yellow/red in analytics.

You get speed without inbox overload. DealerWyze is built for small used car dealers who want to be the first to reply - without making the inbox their office.
