Privacy Policy

DealerWyze — Operated by KMA Auto Inc — Effective Date: May 31, 2026

1. Introduction

KMA Auto Inc ("KMA Auto," "we," "us," or "our") operates DealerWyze, a cloud-based customer relationship management platform for independent used car dealerships ("Service"). DealerWyze is available at dealerwyze.com.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights regarding that information. It applies to all users of the Service, including dealership owners and their staff ("Dealers") and, to the extent described below, the Dealers' own customers whose data is entered into the platform.

By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account and Business Information
When you register for DealerWyze, we collect:

  • Business name, dealership name, and business address
  • Owner name and contact name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Payment and billing information (processed by Stripe; see Section 5.3)
  • Account credentials (email and hashed password)

Dealer Customer Data
Dealers input contact and transaction data about their own customers into the platform. This may include:

  • Customer names, phone numbers, email addresses, and mailing addresses
  • Vehicle interest records and purchase history
  • Appointment and follow-up notes
  • Communication logs (SMS and email threads)
  • Uploaded documents, including copies of driver's licenses, insurance cards, and other dealership paperwork

This customer data belongs to the Dealer (see Terms of Service, Section 9). KMA Auto processes it solely to provide the Service.

Support Communications
If you contact us for support, we retain records of that correspondence.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage and Log Data

  • IP address and approximate geographic location (city/region level)
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages and features accessed, timestamps, and session duration
  • Referring URL

Cookies and Session Tokens
We use cookies strictly for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising cookies, behavioral tracking cookies, or third-party tracking pixels.

Session Replay and Error Tracking
DealerWyze uses Sentry for session replay and error tracking. During normal use, we record a sampled subset (5%) of user sessions to diagnose performance issues, debug errors, and improve the platform. Additionally, all sessions that encounter errors are recorded. Session replays may include customer names, vehicle details, conversation content, and other information visible on your screen. You can opt out of session replay at any time by contacting legal@dealerwyze.com and we will set an opt-out flag in your browser. Session replay data is retained by Sentry for up to 90 days.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Integrations

Google (Gmail OAuth) — If you connect a Gmail account to capture email leads, we request read-only OAuth access scoped to incoming lead-related messages. You can revoke this access at any time through your Google account settings.

Twilio (SMS) — SMS messages sent and received through the Service are processed via Twilio. We store message content, delivery status, timestamps, and phone numbers to maintain your SMS conversation history.

Stripe (Payments) — Subscription billing is processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your full card number, CVV, or bank account details.

Supabase (Data Storage) — All data is stored in Supabase-managed PostgreSQL databases and Supabase Storage (for uploaded files) located in the United States.

Vercel (Hosting) — The DealerWyze application is hosted on Vercel's platform.

Social Media Platforms (Optional Integration) — DealerWyze allows dealerships to connect their social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube) to post vehicle listing videos automatically. When you connect a social account, we receive and store an access token and basic account information (account name, platform user ID) from that platform via OAuth. We use these tokens solely to post content on your behalf when you direct us to do so. We do not read your personal feed, messages, or contact lists. You can disconnect any social account at any time from Settings.

5. SMS Data, TCPA Notice & Customer Opt-In

Dealer customers consent to receive SMS messages through one of the following methods:

  • Inbound text: The customer sends a text message to the dealership's business number, constituting express written consent to receive SMS replies.
  • Inbound call / voice inquiry: The customer calls the dealership's business line. A confirmation SMS is sent only to the number they called from or verbally confirmed during the call.
  • Third-party lead form: The customer submits an inquiry form on CarGurus, AutoTrader, or a similar platform, providing their phone number and requesting contact from the dealership.

Customers may reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from all dealership SMS messages. Reply START to resubscribe. Message and data rates may apply.

The SMS features of DealerWyze are used by Dealers to communicate with their own customers who have opted in to receive messages. As a Dealer:

  • You are responsible for obtaining and maintaining valid consent from your customers before sending SMS messages.
  • KMA Auto does not send marketing SMS messages to your customers.
  • Message delivery is subject to carrier policies and A2P 10DLC registration requirements.

6. Document Storage

DealerWyze allows Dealers to upload sensitive documents, including copies of customer driver's licenses and insurance cards. These documents are stored in access-controlled Supabase Storage, accessible only to authenticated users of your dealership account, and are deleted upon account cancellation at the end of the data retention period.

8. Your Privacy Rights

8.1 California Residents — CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to: know what personal information we collect; request deletion; request correction of inaccurate data; opt out of sale or sharing (we do not sell data); and non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

To submit a request, email: privacy@dealerwyze.com. We will respond within 45 days.

8.2 All Users

All users may access and update account data, request a data export, cancel and request account deletion, revoke Gmail OAuth access, and disconnect any connected social media account at any time from within the DealerWyze application.

9. Security

  • Encryption in Transit: All data is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Encryption at Rest: Data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • Access Controls: Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel with multi-factor authentication.
  • Authentication: User passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.
  • Row-Level Security: Database access policies enforce that each Dealer account can only access its own data.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. Contact us at privacy@dealerwyze.com if you believe we have collected such information.

11. International Users

DealerWyze is operated from the United States and is intended for use by US-based dealerships. All data is stored and processed in the United States.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy
