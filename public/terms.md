## 2. Description of Service

DealerWyze is a subscription-based software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform designed exclusively for independent used automobile dealerships. Features vary by plan and may include:

- Customer contact management and lead tracking (up to 5,000 leads, current and archived)
- BHPH (Buy Here, Pay Here) loan ledger tracking (**record-keeping tool only — we are not a lender**)
- Light bookkeeping and receipt management
- Email integration and automated lead capture

- Vehicle inventory management
- Two-way SMS messaging (up to 1,500 messages/month included)

- Fax (up to 100 pages/month included)
- Photo/document scanning (up to 200 scans/month included)
- Voice AI Assistant — answering calls, capturing leads, scheduling appointments (add-on plan)
- Team management and role-based access

The Service is intended for lawful dealership business operations only. Features available to your account depend on your subscription plan.
