# DealerWyze 60-Second Product Walkthrough Script

## Overview
A short, punchy product video showing the Today dashboard in action. The goal: demonstrate that DealerWyze is simple, fast, and does the work for dealers.

**Duration:** 60 seconds  
**Format:** Loom recording or screen capture  
**Audio:** Spoken narration or silent with text overlays  
**Backdrop:** Desktop/mobile view of the app

---

## Shot-by-Shot Breakdown

### 0:00–0:05 | Hook (5 sec)
**Visual:** Today dashboard loading on screen  
**Narration:** "Every lead matters. But you can't follow them all up at once."  
**Text overlay:** "The Problem"  
**Tone:** Problem-focused, relatable

### 0:05–0:15 | The Solution (10 sec)
**Visual:** Scroll through Today dashboard  
- Show the 3-column layout (needs attention, automated, quiet)
- Point to a single lead card  
- Click to open lead details (name, phone, vehicle, conversation history)  
**Narration:** "DealerWyze puts every lead, every conversation, and what you need to do next in one place."  
**Text overlay:** "One inbox. Clear actions."  
**Tone:** Calm, organized

### 0:15–0:30 | Speed Demo (15 sec)
**Visual:** Quick succession of actions  
1. Click a lead card → opens conversation  
2. Show SMS exchange (dealer texts back in under 60 seconds)  
3. Show customer response  
4. Click "Appt" badge → task created automatically  
5. Pan to conversation history on the side (all context visible)  

**Narration:** "Reply with one tap. Tasks create themselves. You'll never lose a conversation again."  
**Text overlay:** "React in seconds. Never miss follow-up."  
**Tone:** Confident, fast-paced

### 0:30–0:45 | Inventory & Closing (15 sec)
**Visual:** Switch to Inventory section  
- Show vehicle list with photos  
- Show one vehicle's detail page (photos, specs, price, inquiry button)  
- Zoom out to show the public website version  

**Narration:** "Your inventory lives in DealerWyze. When a lead asks about a car, they see photos, price, and specs instantly — on your public dealer website."  
**Text overlay:** "Sell cars in hours, not days."  
**Tone:** Powerful, simple

### 0:45–0:60 | Close & CTA (15 sec)
**Visual:** Return to Today dashboard hero shot  
- Fade to DealerWyze logo/tagline  

**Narration:** "DealerWyze. All your leads, all your inventory, all in one place. Free to try. Start now."  
**Text overlay:** "Stop losing leads. Get DealerWyze."  
**Button/CTA:** "Start Free →" or "Try It Now →"  
**Tone:** Inspirational, simple

## Recording Tips

1. **Use a clean desktop** — minimize distractions, maximize focus on the app
2. **Slow your cursor** — make interactions feel deliberate, not rushed
3. **Show real data** — use actual dealer names, vehicles, and conversations (or realistic mocks)
4. **Narrate clearly** — speak slowly, emphasize key points
5. **Background music** — light, upbeat instrumental (royalty-free)
6. **Subtitles** — add them for accessibility and sound-off viewing
