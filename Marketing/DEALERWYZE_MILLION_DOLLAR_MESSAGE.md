## 3. Sales copy that makes people feel something

Three versions (problem → agitation → solution). First sentence makes the reader feel seen. ~150 words each, 8th grade level.

---

### Version A — “That deal you lost”

You know the feeling. A lead came in last night. You were closed, or busy, or you told yourself you’d reply in the morning. By the time you did, they’d already talked to two other dealers. You didn’t lose that sale because your car was worse. You lost it because someone else replied first.

Small lots can’t afford a six-week CRM rollout. You need one list where every lead lands, every car is attached, and one “Today” view that tells you who needs a reply right now. You need to answer in minutes, with the right vehicle and a real message — not a generic one.

DealerWyze is built for that. One place for every lead, every car, and every next step. Templates that fill in the customer’s name and the exact car so you send a personal reply in a few taps. The dealer who responds at 10:01 wins. This is how you become that dealer.

---

### Version B — “Scattered and sorry”

Your best leads are in Gmail. Some are in texts. Some landed from CarGurus or AutoTrader. You’re not sure which person asked about which car. You mean to follow up. You get pulled away. When you finally get back, they’ve gone quiet.

You’re not bad at selling. You’re drowning in tabs and to-dos. What you need isn’t more willpower — it’s one list. One place where every inquiry shows up, tied to the right vehicle, with a Today view that says who needs your attention now. Reply fast with a message that feels personal, without typing a novel every time.

DealerWyze does that. One inbox. One Today list. Templates that plug in the car, the price, and the link. You stop losing deals to the lot that replied first. You become the one who replied first.

---

### Version C — “Too small for a CRM”

Someone told you you need a “real CRM.” You looked at the big names. They’re built for stores with IT and training time. You’re a small lot. You don’t have six weeks to implement anything. You just need to stop losing leads because you replied late or forgot.

You need one place for every lead and every car. You need to see who’s new and who’s waiting on you — and reply in under five minutes with the right vehicle and a personal-feeling message. No enterprise bloat. No long rollout.

DealerWyze is built for that. One list. One Today view. Fast replies with templates that fill in the details. The first dealer to respond clearly usually gets the conversation. This is how you make sure that dealer is you.

---

**Recommendation:** Use **Version A** as the main sales/landing narrative. It names the exact emotion (that deal you lost) and ties the fix to “first reply wins.”
