## Short value prop (1–2 sentences)

DealerWyze pulls every inquiry — from Gmail, CarGurus, your website, and texts — into one list, ties each person to the car they asked about, and lets you reply in a few taps with a real message and the right link. So you answer fast and stop losing sales to the dealer who replied first.
