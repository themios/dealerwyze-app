# DealerWyze Paid Ads Strategy

## Goal

Acquire qualified independent used-car dealers at low cost by matching high-intent Google searches to a conversion-focused landing page.

Primary objective:
- `Start Free Beta`

Secondary objective:
- `Book a 15-Minute Workflow Review`

## Current Positioning

Primary angle:
- DealerWyze is the dealership operating system that keeps communication running even when the dealer steps away.

What this means in plain language:
- new leads get answered automatically
- follow-up sequences keep running
- BHPH reminders go out automatically
- post-sale review and retention flows trigger automatically
- the product still covers the full dealership stack: texting, leads, BHPH, bookkeeping, pricing, videos, analytics, and voice

## Why This Angle Wins

Older positioning:
- “replace spreadsheets, notes, texts, and emails”

Current stronger positioning:
- “your lot keeps running even when you step away”

Why the current angle is better:
- more specific value
- more urgency
- more differentiation from generic CRM claims
- stronger match for paid search and direct response traffic

## Audience

Best-fit buyer:
- independent used-car dealers
- small dealer teams
- owner-operators
- BHPH or mixed retail/BHPH stores

Operational reality:
- too much lead handling is manual
- communication happens across Gmail, texts, and memory
- follow-ups slip when the day gets busy
- dealer software is often too bloated or too passive

## Core Message Hierarchy

1. Automation is the hook
- the system answers, follows up, reminds, and keeps communication moving

2. Full-system breadth is the proof
- DealerWyze also covers BHPH, books, pricing, recall checks, videos, analytics, and AI tools

3. Practical setup reduces resistance
- Gmail is live quickly
- voice and social require one-time setup
- dealers do not need to replace everything on day one

## Offer

Primary CTA:
- `Start Free Beta`

Secondary CTA:
- `Book a 15-Minute Workflow Review`

Offer points:
- free during beta
- no credit card required
- at least 30 days notice before any paid transition

## Recommended Channel Mix

Start now:
- Google Search only

Do later:
- Meta retargeting after traffic volume exists
- branded retargeting creative after conversion proof exists

Avoid for now:
- broad Meta prospecting
- expensive competitor terms without dedicated competitor pages

## Recommended Landing Page

Primary page:
- `/lp/dealerwyze-os`

Why:
- strongest hook
- strongest category contrast
- better paid-traffic flow
- balances automation with broader dealership-system proof

## Known Product-to-Marketing Notes

These should stay documented because they shape the copy:

- birthday and service reminders require those customer dates to be on file
- social posting and AI voice require per-platform setup
- starter sequences need to be obvious in onboarding

## Success Metrics

Primary:
- qualified beta starts
- booked workflow reviews

Secondary:
- CTR
- cost per qualified lead
- conversion rate on `/lp/dealerwyze-os`
- lead quality by ad group

## Next Recommended Assets

- conversion tracking review
- optional dedicated pages later for:
  - competitor intent
  - BHPH-specific traffic
  - brand-specific traffic
